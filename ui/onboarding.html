How Gia works

Gia runs locally in your browser. It guides 20‑20‑20 breaks and optional longer resets, with privacy‑first on‑device AI tips. Short breaks: every 20 minutes, 20 seconds of voice guidance (or soft notifications if audio is off). Longer resets: you choose 2–10 minutes every 40–120 minutes. Includes an optional wellness tip. Control: pause, resume, or exit anytime from the popup or keyboard shortcut. Phone haptics: open the companion PWA to feel gentle buzz cues during long breaks.
