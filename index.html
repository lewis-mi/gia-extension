Scientific Foundation

Gia is based on the 20-20-20 rule recommended by the American Optometric Association (AOA): take a 20-second break every 20 minutes, looking at something 20 feet away.

Research in Contact Lens & Anterior Eye (2024) found that structured gaze breaks improve tear-film stability and reduce symptoms of fatigue, dryness, and blurred vision.
